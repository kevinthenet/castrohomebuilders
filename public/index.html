Over 20 years of craftmanship

Castro Home Builders is a company that started from the ground up and has been in the Bay Area for over 20 years, building houses from scratch and helping people in the community make the most out of their homes. By remodeling, renovating, and reinvigorating the houses we work on, we are able to provide Bay Area families a fresh start or a new space to enjoy. Castro Home Builders is licensed, bonded, and insured.
